What We Do for You?

Considering the pandemic of COVID-19, we developed a solution for all business types to take them online and serve with the best we can. We provide website, admin panel and Android App for all kind of e-commerce activities and business at most affordable cost.

Our Vision

Our vision is to promote online presence of local market and stores to facilitate our citizens so that we can fight against this pandemic staying at home and availing all facilities at their doorstep. We plan to intergrate your business online, targeting almost all business types, from a big supermarket to a local vendor.
